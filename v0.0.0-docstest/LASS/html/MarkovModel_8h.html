Detailed Description

Discrete-time Markov chain with arbitrary state-value payload.

Authored by Fanbo Xiang and Sever Tipei (UIUC, March 2018). Used by LASSIE's Markov-library editor and by CMOD when expanding events whose children are sampled from a Markov chain rather than a flat distribution.
