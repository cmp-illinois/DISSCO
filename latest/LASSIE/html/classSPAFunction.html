Detailed Description

SPA. A method radio (STEREO/MULTI_PAN/POLAR) determines how many channels are present and how they're labeled; an apply radio (SOUND/PARTIAL) controls whether channel rows can be expanded into per-partial rows. Switching either radio rebuilds the channel list from scratch.

<Fun><Name>SPA</Name> <Method>STEREO|MULTI_PAN|POLAR</Method> <Apply>SOUND|PARTIAL</Apply> <Channels> <Partials>

V

...</Partials>... </Channels> </Fun>
